About

Neverbound Studios is an entertainment company specializing in animation, sound, and videogames.   Neverbound Studios was created in 2013 by Dominic Tallon, a former student of the Art Institute of Pittsburgh.   He currently owns Neverbound Studios and works along with some friends from the Art Institute of Pittsburgh to create fantastic entertainment.
